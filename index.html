Our Policies

Respect

When flying please respect others Pilot, be professional and realistic. Because you are not as you but you as AirAsia Virtual Airlines Family.

Active

Must be 13 years old and have IFC profile, have an active and valid Pro Subscription, Grade 2 or Higher (Access to Training Server or Higher).

Test Flight

You are required to do a pattern flight as a test. You will be contacted by us after joining. This needs to be completed within 7 days, but you may fly and log flight in the meantime.

Communication

Maintain an active presence on Discord to make communication better with other AirAsia Virtual Airlines Pilots.

Pirep & Event

At least once per Month you must fill PIREP (Pilot Report) or Joining Event. Fly only at Training or Expert Server.

Loyalty

Show your commitment with be active person and support our community.
